About Us

Basaveshwara B.Ed College is a reputed institution committed to excellence in teacher education. Located in Basaveshwaranagar, Bengaluru, the college has built a strong foundation in preparing future educators who are professionally competent, ethically grounded, and socially responsible.

Affiliated with a recognized university and approved by NCTE, the college follows a student-centric approach to teaching and learning. Our B.Ed program blends academic theory with practical teaching experience, ensuring that students are well-prepared for real classroom environments.

We are proud to offer:

  • Experienced and dedicated faculty
  • Modern infrastructure with smart classrooms
  • A rich library and digital learning resources
  • Internship and teaching practice in reputed schools
  • Regular seminars, workshops, and cultural activities

Basaveshwara B.Ed College aims to instill a passion for teaching and lifelong learning in every student who walks through our doors.
